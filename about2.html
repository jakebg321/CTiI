My History

My journey into programming started in high school. I purchased a subscription to a service similar to GoDaddy, but for apps. My plan was to sell these apps to small businesses and restaurants. However, this ended up being too expensive. So I decided to teach myself how to code. I watched YouTube videos and read documentation websites. Fast forward to today, I am still programming, but now I focus on creating custom application features and robotics. I am grateful that I learned this valuable skill!
